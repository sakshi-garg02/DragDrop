For all the programmers and coding buffs out there,here is a simple yet challenging game for you all..


Instructions


1. You have been given five questions on the left and one on one empty box on the right of the screen.
2. The answer to the questions is provided at the top.
3. Solve the question, drag the answer, then drop it in the empty box corresponding to the question.
4. If your answer is not correct as per the question, you won't be able to drop it
